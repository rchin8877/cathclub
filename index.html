KEY LEARNING AREAS

Anatomy

Watch youtube videos and take notes on the major coronary arteries. Probably the key area to study before beginning training.

Equipment

Siemens Artis Q is the fluoro system in labs 1-6, with lab 2 being the only bi-plane system. Lab 7 is Canon Alphenix Sky, almost exclusively used for PPMs. Other equipment will be taught during training.
